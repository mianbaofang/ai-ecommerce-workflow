Editorial-grade ecommerce workflow infographic for an open-source AI ecommerce Skill tool. Warm off-white parchment background (like quality paper stock, not bright cream).

Palette: deep burnt amber as primary accent (not candy orange), deep forest green-teal as secondary (a calm mossy color, not vivid blue), muted sage olive green as success and data accents (not vibrant green), muted antique gold for warnings and callouts (not bright yellow), brick terracotta for emergency or urgent items (not bright red), soft lavender-brown for secondary categories (not bright purple), deep warm charcoal text (not pure black). All colors should feel like quality editorial print — slightly desaturated, earthy, mature.

Visual quality: editorial, calm, print-grade. Every color looks slightly desaturated, like quality magazine print. No bright pure-white, no candy color, no neon. No emoji used as icons. No purple gradient or SaaS glow. All icons should be clean mono-style SVGs, not cartoonish.

No people. No copyrighted logos. No private brand marks. No platform brand names like "Taobao" or "Amazon" as readable text. Plain Asian-style typography for Chinese (use Heiti or Songti), Western sans-serif for any numbers.
